Seasonal content is how Forza Horizon 6 keeps endgame players engaged. Missing a season can mean losing exclusive cars.

## Seasonal playlist priority

1. Exclusive car reward events
2. High-payout credit championships
3. Accolade-linked PR stunts
4. Cosmetic-only repeats (low priority)

## Festival badge strategy

- Check badge list at season start
- Group badges by map region to minimize travel
- Use fast travel to festival hubs between clusters

## Showcase and story events

Showcase events often pay large credit bundles. Run them on higher difficulty once your car class matches recommended PI.

## Co-op seasonal tips

- Match car class with friends for role synergy
- Revive teammates in team events when supported
- Share tune codes for seasonal meta cars

## Time-limited pitfalls

- Waiting until last day of season — queue times spike
- Ignoring weekly challenge overlap with seasonal goals
- Selling seasonal reward cars before claiming accolades

## Bottom line

Plan seasonal completion at week one, chase exclusive rewards first, and batch badges by region. Seasonal playlists are the fastest path to rare cars and steady credits.
