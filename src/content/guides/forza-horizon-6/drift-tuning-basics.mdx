Drift events in Forza Horizon 6 reward angle, speed, and combo length. Tuning matters as much as throttle control.

## Drift build foundation

| Setting | Drift direction |
| --- | --- |
| Diff lock (accel) | Higher for sustained angle |
| Camber (rear) | More negative for grip break |
| Tire pressure (rear) | Slightly lower |
| Steering sensitivity | Medium-high |

## Best beginner drift platforms

- V8 muscle (predictable slides)
- 200–400 HP RWD coupes
- Avoid AWD unless using drift conversion kits

## Technique basics

1. Enter corner with controlled lift throttle
2. Initiate slide with light handbrake or clutch kick
3. Maintain angle with throttle modulation
4. Transition smoothly — snappy countersteer only when needed

## Common tuning mistakes

- Too much power — spin out instantly
- Maxed aero on drift cars — kills slide
- Front tires identical to rear on RWD drift builds

## Practice zones

Use open parking lots and marked drift zones before seasonal events. Repeat one corner until transitions feel smooth.

## Bottom line

Start RWD, tune differential and camber first, and add power only after holding 90° corners consistently.
