Credits unlock cars, upgrades, and festival progression. Efficient farming respects event rotations instead of grinding random races.

## Daily credit loop

1. Complete daily and weekly challenges
2. Run seasonal championship on highest paying difficulty you can win
3. Convert skill points at festival hub
4. Sell duplicate low-value cars

## Skill chain farming

| Location type | Method |
| --- | --- |
| Highway | Draft + speed trap chains |
| Open fields | Jump + drift combos |
| City | Near-miss traffic chains |

Use a skill-focused car build with danger sign bonuses if available.

## Event selection tips

- Prioritize events matching your best car class
- Replay high-payout story chapters on hard once tuned
- Co-op seasonal events often pay bonus credits

## Auction house basics

- List cars with popular meta tunes
- Price check before listing — undercutting kills profit
- Do not flip commons — time wasted

## Mistakes to avoid

- Buying every wheelspin offer
- Ignoring weekly seasonal payouts
- Selling rare cars before checking accolade requirements

## Bottom line

Stack daily challenges, seasonal championships, and skill chains on a route you can repeat without crashing. Consistency beats one-off jackpot wheelspins.
