Tool tiers gate biomes. Upgrade tools before weapons when entering new zones.

## Tool priority list

| Tier | Tool | Material |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Flint tools | Meadows |
| 2 | Bronze | Copper + tin |
| 3 | Iron | Swamp crypts |
| 4 | Silver | Mountains |
| 5 | Blackmetal | Plains |

## Weapon note

Clubs and swords follow same material tiers. Craft bow for every biome — kiting saves food.

## Bottom line

Pickaxe upgrades unlock biomes. Rush bronze pickaxe and karve before swamp iron farming.
