Support players win Marvel Rivals by keeping DPS alive long enough to burn enemy Vanguards. Healing numbers matter less than fight-winning saves.

## Positioning triangle

- Stay behind frontline
- Keep two allies in heal range
- Maintain cover within 2 seconds of movement

## Heal priority

1. Ally being focused in active fight
2. Your main Duelist during push
3. Second Vanguard holding objective
4. Yourself — dead supports heal zero

## Ultimate usage

| Situation | Ult type |
| --- | --- |
| Enemy combo incoming | Defensive / cleanse |
| Objective push | Damage amp or AoE heal |
| Stalemate | Counter-initiate with CC support ult |

## Anti-dive habits

- Track enemy flanker spawns on mini-map
- Keep one mobility cooldown for peel
- Call out dives early — prevention beats recovery

## Common support mistakes

- Standing in open to "max HPS"
- Chasing kills while tank dies
- Blowing ult before enemy commits

## Bottom line

Play behind your frontline, heal the focused ally, and save defensive tools for enemy combo windows. Strategists win games by preventing deaths, not topping damage charts.
