Your first Path of Exile 2 character should prioritize survival and clear speed over endgame min-maxing. These builds get you through the campaign without constant deaths.

## Best beginner builds overview

| Build | Class | Why it works |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Lightning Spear Mercenary | Mercenary | Strong ranged clear, simple rotations |
| Frostbolt Sorceress | Sorceress | Safe spacing, good pack damage |
| Earthquake Marauder | Marauder | Tanky melee with straightforward combos |
| Poison Ranger | Ranger | DoT scaling forgives lower weapon DPS early |

## Mercenary Lightning Spear (recommended)

**Core idea:** throw lightning spear for packs, add a single-target skill for bosses.

- Focus on projectile damage and attack speed
- Pick life on gear before fancy affixes
- Link damage support gems as soon as sockets allow

## Sorceress Frostbolt

**Core idea:** frost projectiles slow enemies while you kite.

- Prioritize energy shield + life hybrid nodes early
- Add a secondary skill for boss DPS by Act 2
- Avoid standing still during telegraphed boss slams

## Leveling passive tips

1. Path toward life or defenses first
2. Grab damage clusters only after core survival nodes
3. Use the in-game respec points for early mistakes — save currency respecs for later

## Gear priority checklist

| Priority | Stat |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Life / defenses |
| 2 | Resistance caps (when available) |
| 3 | Movement speed on boots |
| 4 | Weapon DPS or spell damage |

## Bottom line

Pick one published starter build and follow it through Act 3. Mercenary Lightning Spear and Frostbolt Sorceress are the safest on-ramps for new PoE2 players in 2026.
