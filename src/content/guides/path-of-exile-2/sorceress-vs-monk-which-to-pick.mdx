Sorceress and Monk attract different player types. Here is an honest comparison for your first character.

## Quick comparison

| Factor | Sorceress | Monk |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Range | Long | Melee |
| Survivability early | High (kiting) | Medium (requires dodging) |
| Damage style | Spells | Attacks / combos |
| Build complexity | Medium | Medium–high |
| Beginner friendly | Yes | Moderate |

## Sorceress strengths

- Kite enemies while leveling
- Strong elemental spell variety
- Easier boss fights from range
- Forgiving first acts if resistances are okay

## Sorceress weaknesses

- Mana and energy shield management
- Gear dependency for spell scaling
- Slower movement in cramped maps without skills

## Monk strengths

- Satisfying melee combat loop
- Strong combo potential at higher investment
- Feels durable with correct passives and flasks

## Monk weaknesses

- Must read telegraphs closely
- Weapon upgrades matter more often
- Melee positioning punishes new players

## Who should pick Sorceress

- First ARPG / PoE player
- Players who prefer ranged kiting
- Streamers who want stable early progression

## Who should pick Monk

- Experienced melee ARPG players
- Players who enjoy combo timing
- Anyone bored by caster leveling

## Bottom line

Pick **Sorceress** for your first PoE2 character unless you already love melee combat. Pick **Monk** if you accept more deaths while learning attack patterns.
