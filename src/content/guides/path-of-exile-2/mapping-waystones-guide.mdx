Mapping is PoE2's endgame loop. Waystone discipline separates steady currency income from death spirals.

## Pre-map checklist

- Resistances capped
- Life pool comfortable for one-shot avoidance
- Loot filter enabled
- Waystone tier you can clear in under 4 minutes

## Waystone modifiers

| Mod type | Early advice |
| --- | --- |
| Pack size | Good for loot, watch damage |
| Boss damage | Skip until tanky |
| Elemental mods | Match your resist gaps |

## Atlas progression tips

- Push tiers only when clears are consistent
- Complete atlas objectives that boost drop quantity
- Do not chase high tier if dying once per map

## Loot and economy

- Pick up currency always
- Filter out low-value bases
- Vendor recipes still matter mid-league

## Bottom line

Start low-tier waystones, mod conservatively, and push tiers only with stable clears. Mapping is a marathon — sustain beats hero tier pushes.
