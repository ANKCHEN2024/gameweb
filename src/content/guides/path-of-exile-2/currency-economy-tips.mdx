PoE2's economy punishes hoarding and ignorance equally. Early league efficiency is about converting drops into character power — not becoming rich overnight.

## Early league pickup rules

| Item type | Action |
| --- | --- |
| Currency shards/orbs | Always loot |
| Skill gems you use | Keep / upgrade |
| Rare items with life + res | Evaluate for slots |
| White bases | Vendor unless crafting target |

## What not to waste early

- High-tier crafting currency on low-level rares
- Duplicate skill gems with no trade value
- Unique items you do not understand — check before vendoring

## Vendor recipe basics

- Sell item combinations that grant crafting currency (check current league recipes)
- Break down bad rares instead of hoarding full stash tabs

## Trading mindset

1. Fix resistances and life before buying damage gear
2. Trade for a specific affix, not "better gear" in general
3. Price check similar listings before messaging sellers

## Stash discipline

- Tab 1: currency
- Tab 2: gems and leveling gear
- Tab 3: sell/trade rares
- Tab 4: maps / endgame (later)

## Bottom line

Early PoE2 economy is simple: loot currency, upgrade slots that kill you first, and only craft when a upgrade is clearly missing from drops or vendors.
