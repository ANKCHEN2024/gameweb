Early act bosses in Path of Exile 2 are gear checks disguised as skill tests. Preparation beats memorization.

## Universal boss checklist

- Resistances near cap for current act
- Weapon DPS upgraded within last act
- At least one movement skill equipped
- Portal scroll in inventory

## Phase pattern basics

Most early bosses follow:

1. **Auto-attack phase** — learn safe melee range
2. **Telegraphed slam** — dodge lateral, not backward only
3. **Add spawn or aura phase** — clear adds or kite circle
4. **Enrage window** — flask and burst

## Flask timing

- Do not empty all flasks in phase one
- Save two charges for the highest damage phase
- Use utility flasks before entering arena if available

## Melee vs ranged adjustments

| Archetype | Tip |
| --- | --- |
| Melee | Hit twice, reposition — greed kills |
| Ranged | Maintain max range, watch arena edges |
| Caster | Do not channel through slams |

## When to retreat

If you use more than 70% of flasks and boss is above 60% HP, portal out, farm upgrades, return.

## Bottom line

Early act bosses reward preparation: fix resistances, upgrade weapons, and learn one slam pattern. Most "impossible" bosses become routine after a gear pass.
