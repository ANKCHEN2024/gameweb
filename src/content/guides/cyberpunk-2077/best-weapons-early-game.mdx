Weapon choice in early Night City defines how fast you clear gigs and NCPD scanners. Pick one type and specialize.

## Best early weapons by type

| Type | Pick | Why |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Power pistol | Lexington | Common, easy handling |
| Tech rifle | Budget tech rifle | Pierces cover |
| Shotgun | Crush or equal | Close gigs |
| Blade | Any machete | Silent stealth |

## Iconic weapons worth detouring for

Track iconic locations on your map as you explore districts. Iconics scale with iconic upgrade perks later.

## Attribute synergy

- **Reflexes** — pistols and assault rifles
- **Body** — shotguns and LMGs
- **Cool** — snipers and silenced pistols
- **Tech** — tech weapons and smart variants

## Mod and mod slot tips

- Add crit damage when available
- Reload speed helps early survivability
- Do not spread mods across five guns — main two only

## Bottom line

Run Lexington or similar pistol plus one tech or shotgun tool. Specialize mods on two weapons until your build identity locks.
