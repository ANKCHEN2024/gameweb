Night City is huge. Movement efficiency saves real time across gigs, NCPD scans, and Phantom Liberty travel.

## Fast travel basics

- Discover terminals by approaching map icons
- Terminals unlock district-wide travel
- Use map screen to skip redundant driving on repeat routes

## Early vehicle picks

| Vehicle | Use |
| --- | --- |
| Starter bike | Fast gigs, tight alleys |
| Galena | Balanced all-rounder |
| Reward cars | Free power spikes from quests |

## Calling your vehicle

- Bind call vehicle to quick access
- Summon on straight roads — AI pathing struggles in tight clutter
- Switch vehicles at apartment garage when unlocked

## Driving combat tips

- Slow for gunplay windows
- Use vehicle as cover during shootouts
- Do not fight from slow heavy trucks unless armored

## Bottom line

Unlock fast travel terminals early, grab one quest reward vehicle, and use bikes for gig grinding. Driving is optional once terminals cover your routine routes.
