Phantom Liberty is Cyberpunk 2077's biggest content drop. Starting at the right time avoids frustration and keeps the story coherent.

## Recommended start conditions

| Requirement | Why |
| --- | --- |
| Level 25+ | Dogtown enemies hit hard |
| One solid weapon build | Avoid gear scramble |
| Base game quickhacks unlocked | Netrunner tools matter |
| Some legendary cyberware | Survivability buffer |

## Story timing (no spoilers)

- Wait until Johnny/Saburo plot threads are active in main campaign
- Do not rush Dogtown on a fresh level 10 character unless you want hard mode
- Finish a few major Watson/City Center arcs for world context

## Dogtown arrival prep

1. Upgrade armor cyberware slots
2. Stock grenades and healing items
3. Bring a silenced and loud weapon option
4. Save manual backup before first major mission chain

## Relic perk planning

Phantom Liberty introduces relic-based perks. Decide early:

- **Combat relic line** for Sandevistan players
- **Tech/net line** for hackers
- **Hybrid** only if you understand attribute requirements

## What to do first in Dogtown

1. Unlock local fixer and vendors
2. Complete introductory district missions for gear rewards
3. Scan new cyberware before respecing entire build

## Bottom line

Start Phantom Liberty in the mid-to-late campaign with a focused build and level 25+. Treat Dogtown as a power spike chapter, not a side detour for under-leveled characters.
