Bloodpoints unlock perks, items, and addons. Efficient farming respects match objectives instead of sandbagging teammates.

## Daily routine

1. Complete all daily rituals
2. Reroll low-value rituals if allowed
3. Queue with BP offerings equipped
4. Play 2–3 focused matches rather than tilt-queue all night

## Survivor BP build ideas

- **Prove Thyself** + gen rush coop
- **We're Gonna Live Forever** + safe saves
- **Leader** + cooperative healing
- Bring toolbox only if gen scoring is the goal

## Killer BP build ideas

- **BBQ & Chilli** for cross-map pressure scoring (if owned)
- **Pop Goes the Weasel** for gen regression points
- Chase often but end chases when gen pressure demands

## Event stacking

During seasonal events:

- Equip event consumables every match
- Stack party offerings in SWF if coordinated
- Prioritize archives challenges that overlap rituals

## What not to do

- Intentionally derank teammates — reports and bans
- Ignore objectives for "meme" farming
- Burn premium offerings on bad ping lobbies

## Long-term efficiency

| Goal | Method |
| --- | --- |
| Perk unlocks | Focus one character until all perks tier III |
| General grind | Alternate survivor/killer dailies |
| Event pass | Stack challenges + offerings |

## Bottom line

Rituals + offerings + objective-heavy play beats AFK strats. Farm bloodpoints by playing normal matches well, not by throwing games.
