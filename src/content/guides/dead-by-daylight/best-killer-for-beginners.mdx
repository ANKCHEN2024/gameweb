Killer gameplay in Dead by Daylight is the steepest learning curve. Start with simple powers before jumping to high-APM killers.

## Best beginner killers ranked

| Killer | Difficulty | Lesson taught |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wraith | Easy | Map control and surprise hits |
| Trapper | Easy | Zone control and trap mind games |
| Hillbilly | Medium | Macro pressure with chainsaw |
| Huntress | Medium | Ranged pressure and aim |
| Nurse | Hard | Skip until fundamentals solid |

## Wraith starter kit

- Use invisibility to approach loops, not open fields
- Learn when to uncloak for speed vs surprise
- Run basic chase perks: Jolt, Bamboozle, or Pain Resonance

## Trapper fundamentals

- Place traps at strong loops, not random grass
- Respect pallet breaks before committing long chases
- Accept some survivors will respect traps — rotate gens

## Hillbilly when ready

- Chainsaw for cross-map pressure, not every chase
- Learn overheat management
- Punish greedy unhooks with map presence

## Universal killer tips

1. Hook pressure > tunneling every game
2. Break strong pallets early on high-traffic loops
3. Listen for injured grunts and gen regressions

## Bottom line

Start with **Wraith** or **Trapper**, learn loop pressure and gen defense, then graduate to Hillbilly. Save Nurse and blink killers until you win chases consistently on M1 fundamentals.
